NUPI CV
Cristiana Maglia

Political Scientist

Hi! I am a senior research fellow at the Norwegian Institute of International Affairs (NUPI). I am currently the post-doctoral researcher of the project Ad hoc crisis response and international organisations (ADHOCISM), funded by the Research Council of Norway.

I hold a PhD in Political Science (2020) from the Federal University of Rio Grande do Sul (UFRGS), Brazil. My areas of expertise include global governance, international organizations, comparative politics and Brazilian political institutions.

In this page, I will share some of my projects!

'Ad hoc coalitions in global governance: short-notice, task-and time-specific cooperation' with Yf Reykers, John Karlsrud, Malte Brosig, Stephanie Hofmann and Pernille Rieker. International Affairs, 99:2.

'Global networks to national governance? Changings of professional expertise in Amazon environmental governance'. Global Networks, 22:4.
